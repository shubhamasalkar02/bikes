BMW

Overview The BMW S1000RR is a high-performance, liter-class supersport motorcycle produced by BMW Motorrad. First introduced in 2009, it has undergone significant updates, solidifying its position as a top contender in the superbike segment. Key Features
1. Engine: 999cc, inline-4, DOHC, 4-valve, water-cooled engine producing 207 horsepower (155 kW) at 13,500 rpm.
2. Transmission: 6-speed, sequential, manual transmission with ShiftCam technology.
3. Electronics: Advanced rider aids, including Dynamic Traction Control (DTC), ABS Pro, and Cornering ABS.
4. Suspension: Inverted 45mm fork and rear monoshock.
5. Brakes: Dual 320mm front discs, 6-piston Brembo monobloc calipers, and a 220mm rear disc.
6. Weight: 449 pounds (204 kg)
7. Seat Height: 32.3 inches (820 mm)
